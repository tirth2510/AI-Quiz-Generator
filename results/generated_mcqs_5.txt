## MCQ 1
**Question:**  What is the name of the theory explaining planet formation?
A) Interstellar Cloud Theory 
B) Protostar Hypothesis
C) Nebular Hypothesis 
D) Accretion Theory
**Correct Answer:** C

## MCQ 2 
**Question:** What type of celestial body is a planet generally required to orbit?
A) Black hole
B) Asteroid
C) Star

**Correct Answer:** C
