## MCQ 1
Question: What is the most widely accepted theory for planet formation?
A) Planetary Collision Theory 
B) Nebular Hypothesis
C) Stellar Evolution Theory
D) Accretion Disk Model
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question:  What characteristic differentiates a planet from a star or brown dwarf?
A) Size 
B) Composition
C)  Orbit
D) Temperature 
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3 
Question: Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of a planet?
A) Rounded shape
B) Orbits a star, stellar remnant, or brown dwarf
C) Emits its own light
D) Formed through accretion 
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 4
Question:  Which of the following is a terrestrial planet in our solar system?
A) Jupiter
B) Saturn 
C) Uranus
D) Mars
Correct Answer: D

## MCQ 5
Question: What is the process called by which planets grow within a protoplanetary disk?
A) Sublimation
B)  Condensation
C)  Accretion
D) Fission
Correct Answer: C
